    About Team 2

    Team 2 is dedicated to developing innovative solutions for quality control in pottery and ceramics manufacturing. Our multimodal sensor fusion system combines thermal imaging, acoustic analysis, and computer vision to detect surface and subsurface defects in greenware clay with unprecedented accuracy.

    We work closely with local artisans, pottery workshops, and industrial manufacturers to ensure our technology respects traditional craft practices while delivering modern quality assurance capabilities.
